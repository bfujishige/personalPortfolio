About Us

Ground up is a coffee shop dedicated to serving quality coffee. Founded by FirstLast1 and FirstLast2, both from Montebello, CA. The idea for the shop came when we were at a coffee shop in Pasadena, and we thought, "Why don't we have a place like this in our area?" So we siezed the opportunity and founded Ground Up Coffee in Montebello, CA on Garfield blvd and Via Campo, right next to J&S.
